Find your perfect mentor

Looking for a mentor? You’re not alone. Most of us want to know the secrets to building a relationship with someone who can advise our career path, answer our on-the-job questions, and maybe even help us land the next big thing.

Ask for help on important topics

Inquiring about past instances of failure with your mentor can be a great technique for establishing a good rapport. It’s a great question because it leaves ample room for an open-ended response.

Research other questions

By inquiring about something pertaining to their career, you are demonstrating an interest in them. The question appeals to the notion that most people enjoy speaking about themselves, and if you can somehow tie the question into something that directly pertains to their career.
